SHORT BIOGRAPHY

A traditional voice in a contemporary field of silence.

Danial HM is an Iranian composer and Persian setar artist based in Austria. His music brings the intimate voice of the setar into ambient, cinematic and contemporary instrumental settings, creating spaces shaped by restraint, resonance and focused listening.

Rather than treating silence as an absence, his work allows it to become part of the composition. Each phrase is given room to breathe, connecting Persian musical memory with a personal language of stillness, nature and emotional awareness.
