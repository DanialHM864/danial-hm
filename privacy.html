Essential website functions

The site can be used without accepting analytics. Local storage is used to remember your privacy choice so the consent message does not appear on every visit.

Optional Google Analytics

Google Analytics 4 is activated only after you select “Accept analytics.” It is used to understand general website traffic, page engagement and referral sources. Advertising storage and advertising personalization remain disabled.

Your choice

You may accept or decline analytics. You can reopen the consent controls at any time through “Privacy settings” in the footer.

External links

The website links to third-party services such as YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, Instagram and PayPal. Their own privacy policies apply after you follow those links.
