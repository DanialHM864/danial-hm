Danial HM

Danial HM is an Iranian composer and Persian setar artist based in Austria. His music explores silence, memory, inward movement and the fragile moment in which sound becomes emotional presence.

At the center of his work is the Persian setar: not as a decorative symbol of tradition, but as a living voice. Through improvisation, restraint and close attention to resonance, he allows the instrument to speak naturally within ambient, cinematic, world and contemporary instrumental music.

Artistic philosophy

Every composition begins with listening. Silence is treated as structure, emotion comes before display, and each musical gesture must earn its place. The goal is not to overwhelm the listener, but to create enough space for the listener to return to themselves.

“Music does not need to fill every moment. Sometimes its meaning begins where the sound ends.”
