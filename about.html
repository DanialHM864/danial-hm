THE ARTIST

Danial HM

Danial HM is an Iranian composer and Persian setar artist based in Austria. His work explores silence, memory, inward movement and emotional depth through contemporary instrumental music.

At the center of his artistic language is the Persian setar—not as decoration or historical reference, but as a living narrative voice capable of speaking naturally within ambient, cinematic and world music.

Artistic philosophy

Every composition begins with listening. Silence is treated as structure, emotion comes before virtuosity, and nothing remains without purpose.

“Music does not need to fill every moment. Sometimes its meaning begins where the sound ends.”
