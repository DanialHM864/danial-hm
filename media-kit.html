50-WORD BIO

Danial HM is an Iranian composer and Persian setar artist based in Austria. His ambient, cinematic and contemplative instrumental music brings Persian musical memory into a contemporary language shaped by silence, restraint, resonance and attentive listening. He releases music independently through Danial HM Records.

100-WORD BIO

Danial HM is an Iranian composer and Persian setar artist based in Austria. Working between Persian musical heritage and contemporary ambient, cinematic and instrumental music, he creates intimate listening spaces shaped by breath, resonance and silence. His work treats the setar not only as a historical instrument, but as a living artistic voice capable of expressing modern emotional experience. Rather than forcing dramatic conclusions, his compositions often unfold through patience and attentive presence, allowing each phrase to determine the next. Danial HM releases his work independently through Danial HM Records.
